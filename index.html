Kawasaki

Here are some things to know about Kawasaki motorcycles: History Kawasaki Heavy Industries started producing motorcycle engines in 1953, and the first complete Kawasaki motorcycle was produced in 1954. Kawasaki has produced many iconic motorcycles, including the 500SS Mach-III, Z1 (900 Super Four), Ninja (GPz900R), and the supercharged Ninja H2. Design philosophy Kawasaki's design philosophy is called RIDEOLOGY, which stands for "Ride + Ideology". The philosophy is based on the idea that Kawasaki motorcycles should enrich the lives of people around the world.
